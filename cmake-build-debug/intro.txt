Welcome to SpaceXplorer!
In this game, the goal is to destroy aliens to score points
To destroy aliens (@) you need to drive in to them
But be warned! Bumping in to aliens will damage your ship!
And driving around in space consumes fuel!
However, there is no need to worry!
Collect space junk (*) and convert it to fuel or repair your ship
Beware of the asteroids (A) traveling across space, they will result in instant death!
To complete this quest, the player will need to survive 100 turns and his score will be logged on the leader board.
